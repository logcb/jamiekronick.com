Michaëlle Jean, former Governor General of Canada

Ottawa Citizen Style Magazine, Spring 2012

I was given carte blanche to photograph Michaëlle however I saw fit. As Governor General, Michaëlle had been photographed thousands of times, so I chose to show the behind the scenes of a 'standard' headshot photograph and bring a human element to an otherwise typical photograph.

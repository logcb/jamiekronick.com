Jamie Kronick is a fine art and commercial photographer in Ottawa, Canada.

His work, predominantly rooted in portraiture, strikes a balance between people and place. Combining his love of music and the arts with his need to document the relationship of subject and environment, his work brings you a glimpse into the lives of others.
